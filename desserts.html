Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate and let it cool slightly.
  2. Whip the cream until soft peaks form.
  3. Beat egg whites with salt until foamy, then gradually add sugar and beat to stiff peaks.
  4. Mix egg yolks and vanilla into the melted chocolate.
  5. Fold in whipped cream, then gently fold in egg whites.
  6. Spoon into serving dishes and chill for at least 2 hours.

Fruit Trifle

Ingredients:

  • 1 sponge cake, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups mixed fresh fruits (berries, bananas, kiwi, etc.)
  • 2 cups vanilla custard
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1/4 cup fruit juice or sherry (optional)

Steps:

  1. Layer half the cake cubes in a trifle bowl and drizzle with juice or sherry if using.
  2. Add a layer of mixed fruits, then half the custard.
  3. Repeat layers with remaining cake, fruits, and custard.
  4. Top with whipped cream and garnish with extra fruit.
  5. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
